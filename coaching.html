1. Initial Assessment & Goal Setting

Purpose: Introduction, information gathering, identifying current strengths, challenges, and priorities.

Outcome: A personalized coaching plan with clear, measurable goals.

2. Skill-Building Sessions

Structure: Typically weekly (50 minutes), one-on-one

Focus Areas:

  • Time management & prioritization
  • Back Mapping & transitions
  • Task initiation & follow-through
  • Organization systems
  • Planning & breaking down long-term projects
  • Flexible thinking & problem-solving
  • Self-monitoring & accountability
